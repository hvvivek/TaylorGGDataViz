Working Mother's Top Rated Companies for Women Ranked Best to Worst

How does better company paid maternity leave reflect female representation in those companies?

Best Companies application includes more than 400 questions on leave policies, workforce representation, benefits, childcare, advancement programs, flexibility policies and more. It surveys the availability and usage of these programs, as well as the accountability of the many managers who oversee them. Company profiles and data come from submitted applications and reflect 2016 data.

Advancement Score: Average of percentage of female employees who participate in career counseling, percent participating in management/leadership training, and percent of how many participate in mentoring

Support Score: Average of if company offers childcare and if company subsidizes backup eldercare.

Maternal Score: How much paid maternity leave is given compared to best company's policy of 26 weeks.

Representation Score: Average of % of employees who are women, % of managers/executives who are women, and new hires who are women.

Source"Working Mother's 2017 Best Companies"
